Militia

The Planetary Defense Militia of the Aurora-4 system was established by the council in a desperate bid to ensure safety across the industrial worlds. These local forces maintain a strong alliance with the newly arrived Imperial reinforcements, sharing a unified commitment to safeguarding the region.
