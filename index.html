This website is an announcement for an upcoming browser game set in the dark, futuristic depths of space.

Setting

"The Imperium of Man has expanded to the farthest reaches of the Milky Way Galaxy. In this totalitarian, xenophobic society, every individual is merely a resource to be exploited in the name of the immense political structure that dominates every corner of known space. To maintain order and security, the Imperium must keep billions of soldiers in its army. Countless military formations face constant threats from alien invaders, ruthless pirates, and rebels fighting to establish their own regimes."
