Lore

The entire story unfolds within the planetary system of Aurora-4, where humanity once established industrial colonies and infrastructure. However, due to unexplained anomalies and intense "space storms," the Imperium lost contact with these distant worlds. Now, one hundred years later, the unified military forces have decided to launch an expedition, taking advantage of a temporary clearing in the path to this mysterious system.
