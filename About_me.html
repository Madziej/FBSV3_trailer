About me

My coding journey began in January 2024 with my first programming language, Java. Driven by a desire to understand all aspects of programming, I expanded my focus after a few months to include frontend development. The knowledge I possess has been earned through countless hours spent alone in front of a computer after my regular 9-to-5 work shifts. Most of my learning comes from a combination of Udemy courses and free YouTube lessons shared by independent tutors. I must admit, my determination to keep going is fueled by the daily frustrations of life, which act as a kind of rocket fuel for my progress.
