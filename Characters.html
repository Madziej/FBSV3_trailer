Playable Characters

I. Knights of the Galaxy

These superhuman warriors serve the Imperium of Man with unwavering loyalty. Clad in mechanized armor and empowered by altered DNA, they possess enhanced strength and stamina beyond ordinary human limits. To withstand harsh environments and severe injuries, their bodies are equipped with extra organs, such as a second heart and additional lungs. These formidable champions are the ultimate danger to any foe of the Human Dominion.

Imperial Soldiers

The Imperial Soldiers form the largest organized fighting force across the galaxy, acting as the Imperium of Man's primary military arm and first line of defense against countless dangers threatening humanity’s survival. Their combat tactics resemble those of traditional armies from our own world. Although fundamentally a conventional human military, they wield some futuristic weaponry—such as laser carbines—and command imposing artillery tanks.

Tech Monks

These unconventional warriors are heavily augmented with mechanical implants that give them an almost inhuman appearance. They hold the belief that replacing parts of their organic bodies with artificial enhancements brings them closer to perfection. For them, salvation lies in gathering and preserving ancient knowledge, all in the name of humanity. A primary benefit of their cybernetic enhancements is the addition of extra limbs, enabling them to launch multiple attacks within each turn in combat.
